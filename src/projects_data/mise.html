It's the year 2025, I love cooking, and I am tired of my scattered collection of bookmarked recipes.

Mise En Place is a website I built using Laravel & Vue to solve this issue. With it, you can create or import recipes from your favourite sites (with attribution of course!). All of the recipes you care about in one place, in one format, editable as you please.

It is still in active development, and likely will always be. Oh, it is also public and free, give it a shot!
