A fun and short retro shooter, with unpredictable controls.

Initially developed in Unity (C#) for HackSussex’s GameJam (2023), where it won in two categories: “most unconventional controls” & “best overall”.
I then re-wrote the entire project using Godot, because it's fun to learn.

You can (and should) play it here .
